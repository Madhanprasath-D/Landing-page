Our AI Phishing Blocker project combines cutting-edge technologies to provide you with a robust defense against phishing attempts while navigating the vast online landscape.

Overview

The cybersecurity landscape is constantly evolving, and so are the tactics of malicious actors. Phishing, one of the most insidious cyber threats, involves deceptive techniques to trick users into revealing sensitive information. Our project addresses this issue head-on by employing a potent blend of Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) and machine learning (ML). Through these technologies, we have crafted a browser extension that acts as a real-time guardian, instantly analyzing URLs for potential phishing indicators. By doing so, we empower users to browse with confidence and thwart potential phishing attempts.
